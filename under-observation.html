Under Observation

Under Observation is the only game I've published on Steam. I worked with a few talented artists, but I programmed almost everything myself, even though I wasn’t experienced in game development before that. I was also the team lead responsible for guiding the team through the development. It’s a little rough around the edges, but it is very playable nonetheless.

Being one of the first big projects I worked on, I consider working on Under Observation a pivotal learning experience. I learned how much work actually goes into developing certain features, and it gave me a good sense of scope for future projects. My Unity and C# skills improved significantly during the development of Under Observation.

In Under Observation, you assume the role of a scientist who’s trapped inside an underground laboratory. Companying you is a research subject gone rogue, which you’ll have to research and avoid until you can escape the facility.
