Personal Game Projects

Portfolio Course

Over the summer of 2023, I worked on my personal projects and enrolled on a portfolio course, where I got to work on multiple different projects. Fog Island is a study on procedural terrain generation, where each time you jump in, you find yourself on a different island. Pathfinding Study lets you test different types of pathfinding algorithms on a 16x16 grid. Nodes is a proof of concept for a factory builder game.

The biggest project I worked on during this time was Tank Builder. The player can design and build tanks using a block-based system similar to one used in a PC game, RoboCraft. After building the tank, the player can drive it around a test course.
