Viis Kakone

Viis Kakone was developed using Unreal Engine and created as part of our 3D game development course. In this project, I took my first steps developing games on Unreal Engine and programmed stuff related to data persistence and procedural generation. I actually came up with the idea for the game, so I also got to make a lot of design choices in this project.

In Viis Kakone, you are presented with a cafe filled with various items. The goal of the game is to memorize the position of each item so you can rearrange the cafe after an earthquake that has knocked everything down.
