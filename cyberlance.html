Cyberlance

CyberLance was the last school project I worked on. Here, instead of coming up with our own game idea, the premise and the platform were given to us by a publisher. The prompt we were given was “Make a PC game where you play as a medic in a cyberpunk city.” I once again assumed the role of a code lead.

The immediate thought that came to everyone’s mind was to make a driving game where you have to drive around the city in an ambulance, picking up patients along the way. To spice things up, you would have to perform procedures on the patient that come in the form of on-screen mini games.

During the development, I designed and programmed the HUD UI, made a couple of mini games, did some 3D modeling, designed sounds, and was very involved in the overall design of the game.
