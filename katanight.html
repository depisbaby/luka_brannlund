KataNight

KataNight was developed as part of our mobile game development course. The game was published on Google Play, and I’d consider it to be one of the most polished products I’ve had the pleasure of working on.

Once again, I assumed the role of a code lead and planned out the initial technical direction for the game. I got to do a lot of UI related programming during the project and ultimately learned how games are developed on mobile.

In KataNight, you, as a player, face endless waves of enemies that have to be defeated with specific gesture combinations. The game takes heavy inspiration from One Finger Death Punch.
